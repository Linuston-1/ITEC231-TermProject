CENSORSHIP IN MEDIA: GLOBAL PERSPECTIVES

Film Censorship in Iran

History

After the end of the Iranian revolution ended in 1979, many new rules were put in place to limit the artistic expression of Iranian filmakers. These limitations include women not being allowed to appear without a hajab and sexual content not being allowed to be shown on camera. Film-makers have been arrested for showing these things on camera and there has been large scale foreign activism to try and raise awareness to the issue.



Hijab ban

Actresses are not allowed to appear without Hijabs and many have been punished for doing so.



Sexual content

Any sign of affection or sexual content is banned from the screen.
