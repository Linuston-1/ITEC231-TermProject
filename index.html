CENSORSHIP IN MEDIA: GLOBAL PERSPECTIVES

While most people enjoy modern media every day of their life, many do not realize that what they are seeing is often censored in some way. Censorship is often seen as conflicting with freedom of expression, but often censorship is relatively benign and deals mostly with age ratings.

That does not mean that actual censorship of opposing ideas does not occur, however. Many governments around the world, including ones considered "free" censor media that their citizens see.
